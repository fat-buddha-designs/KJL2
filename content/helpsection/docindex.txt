Title: KJL Solicitors CMS Help
----
Text:

Kirby is a simple but powerful CMS, most tasks can be completed from the front page of the CMS.

Use the left hand menu for details on adding pages or news articles etc.

To get back to the front page of the CMS click on the menu in the top-bar of the page and go to 'Site'.

For help click on the menu in the top-bar of the page and go to 'Help' and follow the guidance there.
