Having effective and relevant terms and conditions are crucial in order to protect your business and its services. 

Our solicitors can help you safeguard your company with clear and comprehensive terms and conditions that contain provision for:-

- Interest on late payment
- Limitation against potential claims brought against you resulting from your breach
- Retention of title
- Time limits or limitation of time on the performance of the contract
- Your chosen jurisdiction
- Protection of your business against:
- Unfair competition
- Poaching of staff
- Employee misconduct
- Termination of contracts based on your terms
- Your remedies clearly defined should your customer or supplier be in breach
- Compliance with all the Consumer Regulations and laws if applicable
- Compliance with Health & Safety, The Housing Grants Act & limitations on 3rd party default as and where appropriate. 

If you have a question then please do not hesitate to contact us for advice at KJL Solicitors, Blofield Chambers, The Street, Blofield, Norwich, NR13 4AA. 

We are conveniently based just outside Norwich within minutes of the A47 and with parking right outside our offices.
